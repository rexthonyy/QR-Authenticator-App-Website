Built-in Security

From inception, QR Authenticator was built to help you cut down on your business costs, increase your productivity, improve your operations or live a better life, which is why security was placed as the top most priority.

QR Authenticator end-to-end encryption is available when you and your contacts exchange data. This ensures that only you and your contacts have access to the information that is being sent and nobody in between. And since the data is not sent online via the internet, issues arising from a remote hack are non existent as the data is encrypted and sent directly between the two devices and nobody in between, not even QR Authenticator. This is because each contact data is secured with a lock and only the recipient and you have the special key needed to unlock the information stored in the data. For added protection, every time that a contact is transferred, a new unique lock and key is created. All of this happens automatically and is built into the app.
