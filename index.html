QR Authenticator App

QR Authenticator is a free to download app that was built to facilitate the super fast and secure authentication of your contacts. It's really simple. Scan to add your contacts to a category and scan to authenticate a contact. That's it! Manage your contacts by using tools to easily sort and share your contacts with others.

QR Authenticator can also help businesses cut down on the costs of printing receipts, tickets or coupons. Learn more about QR Authenticator features.

END-TO-END ENCRYPTION

Security by Default

Some of your most important contacts are stored on QR Authenticator which is why we built end-to-end encryption. When end-to-end encrypted, your details are securely transferred directly to the person receiving it and nobody in between not even QR Authenticator.
