About QR Authenticator

Our Mission

QR Authenticator started as an alternative to the conventional means of physical authentication using paper and pencil. The product now allows businesses and people to improve their operations and increase their efficiency. The contacts are secured with end-to-end encryption, meaning that no third party including QR authenticator can get access to them. Behind every product decision is our desire to improve peoples lives by providing them with the tools needed to make tomorrow better than today.

Our Team

QR Authenticator was built by Rex Anthony, a software developer who had spent more than 5 years in software development, as a way for businesses and people to cut down on costs and increase productivity.
