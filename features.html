Simple, secure authentication

Authenticate your party guests, business acquaintances or customers for free, without the need for an internet connection so you can save your data costs.

Share contacts

Send your contacts on QR Authenticator instantly to others. QR Authenticator encodes your contacts into QR codes and does not use your phone's internet connection so you don't have to worry about data charges.

Manage contacts

Rather than incurring the costs of printing tickets, coupons or invitations then handing them out to your contacts, with QR Authenticator, you can manage your contacts by placing them in categories and get access to them right on your device which saves you time and money.

END-TO-END ENCRYPTION

Security by Default

Some of your most important contacts are stored on QR Authenticator which is why we built end-to-end encryption. When end-to-end encrypted, your details are securely transferred directly to the person receiving it and nobody in between not even QR Authenticator.

QR and Barcode Scanner

Scan all types of QR and bar codes instantly using the in built scanner with autofocus functionality.
